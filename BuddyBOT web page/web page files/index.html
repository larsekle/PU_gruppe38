Features of BuddyBOT

Assistance while coding

BuddyBOT assists students taking programming courses at NTNU, by providing online resources about parts of the programming language the student finds challenging.

BuddyBOT will contact the student when a sufficient amount of errors are generated. The student can then choose between several links about the theme, already rated by other students to provide the best possible information and help. In addition, students can contact BuddyBOT if help is needed beside the automatic appearance of BuddyBOT.

Helpful resources

Graphical report

Lecturers and student assistants can get reports about their students programming skills by creating a user at buddybot.com. This makes it easier for student assistants to help each student more accurate, and for the lecturers to customize their classes.

About us

As computer engineering students we have taken multiple programming courses at NTNU the last couple of years. Finding it hard to define what our main struggle is when doing assignments and spending hours trying to get help from the busy study assistants, we came up with our idea: a digital study assistant.

BuddyBOT will guide students smoothly through thir assignments and help both study assistants and lecturers in there everyday working life. Let BuddyBOT help you now!
